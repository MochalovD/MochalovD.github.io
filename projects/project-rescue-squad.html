Blockout "Rescue Squad"

This project was inspired by stealth adventure games like Uncharted, Metal Gear Solid and The Last Of Us.

I want to use maximum of AGLS framework and practice my level design skills.

Engine: UE 5.6

Status: In progress

LDD: Ready, in translation phase.

Moodboard: https://www.pinterest.com/dmtimemanager/resque-squad/

Gameplay

Map Layout Overview

Top down overview of the player path and progress

Zones

Starting location - prison. The player gets used to controls and trying to figure out what's goin on.

After exiting the prison - player engage with 2 enemies and finds that elevator did not work anymore. He needs to find another path to climb the rock.

At the top here we will enter Warehouse, which has a few enemies and 3 different path to go through it.

When player exit warehouse he clearly sees his current main destination - Kitchen. But here again he has 3 completely different ways to reach it.

After kitchen, he needs to collect 2 access cards to enter the Lab. So he goes to Administration building and Boss house in any order.

In Lab he has 1 blocked path(final exit from the underground) and 2 path to the main lab. Right path will give high ground advantage with good overview.

He can escape lab through a small opening in the back wall. Using grappling hook he can jump over the abyss to the exit path.

Here outside of the main base he need to run to the evacuate point. But it's not that simple...
