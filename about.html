Dmitrii Mochalov

From a very early age I was fascinated by games — at first it was Warcraft II, Blood, and Command & Conquer. Later that shifted to Warcraft III and Counter-Strike, but what truly captured my heart were multiplayer games — Dota, Lineage, Ragnarok Online, FF14, and eventually World of Warcraft.

Sometimes I try mobile games that hook me for one reason or another. The most recent was AFK Journey, which has incredible visuals and a compelling core loop that keeps you coming back every day.

I like a systematic approach to everything, so I don’t just play games — I study their mechanics and systems. I’m also a big fan of Dungeons & Dragons 5e, whose rules I’ve learned thoroughly.

After joining Wargaming I realized once and for all that I don’t want to do anything other than game development. So right now my growth is fully focused on game design and building prototypes in Unreal Engine 5 as my main engine. I also plan to return to Unity to refresh the basics for rapid prototyping, and to learn Unreal Editor for Fortnite (UEFN) to broaden my horizons.

My technical university background and prior experience in various IT roles help me pick up new technologies quickly, so I consider myself someone who can switch between them with ease.
